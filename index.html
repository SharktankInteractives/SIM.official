THE IMPERIAL WAY

SIM takes place on the Death Star during the early ages of the Empire. Before the transition to Stormtrooper armor, TK Trooper armor was used by the soldiers of the Empire. The game focuses on the day-to-day operations and management of the Death Star Reactor and Superlaser as well as overall security of the Battlestation. There will be ingame teams that manage logistics, power supply, coolant as well as operators managing the consoles of power usage. The reactor must be maintained to allow the battlestation to have enough power supply, while the other department works on keeping the superlaser stable.

OUR FORCES

IFT

Imperial Flametroopers

Ensuring specialized fire support and hazardous terrain neutralization. The IFT is the Empire's primary scorched-earth deterrent.

IST

Imperial Shock Troopers

The elite guard of high-value infrastructure and citizen safety. They are the visible shield of the Imperial capital.

ERG

Emperor's Royal Guard

The peak of martial prowess. Chosen for their unwavering loyalty and lethal precision, guarding the highest levels of the Empire.

ICSU

Imperial Commando Special Unit

Executing high-stakes operations that require surgical precision. The ICSU operates where standard infantry cannot reach.

VF

Vader's Fist

The legendary 501st Legion. Lead directly by the highest command to spearhead the Empire's most vital conquests.

ISB

Imperial Security Bureau

Intelligence and Law Enforcement. Dedicated to internal safety and the root-cause analysis of galactic instability.
